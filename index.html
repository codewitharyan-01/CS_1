Our Story

From Bhavnagar to Your Heart

What started as a small dream in Bhavnagar has now become Nikol's favorite street food destination. Bunty Kaka brought the authentic Kathiawadi flavors to Ahmedabad with his signature Bateka Bhungla.

Every dish is prepared with the same traditional recipe passed down through generations. The secret lies in the perfect blend of spices and the love we put into every serving.
